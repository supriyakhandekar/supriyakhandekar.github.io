Hi, I'm Supriya.

Nice to meet you.


Last December, I fell in love with CS. Ever since, I've plunged into the field quickly and relentlessly, seizing opportunity after opportunity to engage, learn, and grow as a young computer scientist. Within the recent spring semester, I attended engineering conferences, hackathons, got involved on the board for Women in CS at Columbia, worked with data analytics at a internship in downtown Manhattan, and landed a challenging internship at Yahoo for the summer.


I've always wondered how CS hooked me, and I soon came to the realization that I am attracted to two main things: it's challenge and intersectionality.


The amount of possibility in this field is exciting. I can't wait to learn more and build more. This is just the start, but it's going to be a great ride.
